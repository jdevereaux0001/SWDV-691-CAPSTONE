About Us

Blazing Trails Side-by-Side Club was formed in 2023 when a group of off-roading enthusiast friends decided to brave the trails of Hatfield & McCoy's in Matewan, WV. Since then, we've been in love with the outdoors and offroads, and our sole purpose is to share our love with others.
